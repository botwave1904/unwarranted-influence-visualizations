About This Book

Every factual claim in this book is sourced to a document: a declassified government filing, a court docket, a congressional report, an SEC disclosure, an ethics filing, or peer-reviewed historical scholarship. No anonymous sources. No hearsay.

The book reconstructs, from primary documents, the operation of American legal, political, and commercial institutions across the period 1933 to 2026. The documents span 931 years — from the Council of Clermont in 1095 to the present — arranged to show the continuity of institutional power.

On Method

  • Every citation is reproducible
  • No URL cited that was not personally verified
  • No claim made without a document
  • Where the record is ambiguous, the text says so
